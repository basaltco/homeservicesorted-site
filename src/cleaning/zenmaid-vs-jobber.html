ZenMaid vs Jobber: Which Is Better for Your Maid Service? (2026)

ZenMaid is built specifically for residential maid services. Jobber is the strongest all-rounder for any cleaning or field service business. The comparison isn't about which tool is better overall — it's about which one fits where you are right now. Small residential maid service with budget constraints? ZenMaid. Growing business that needs online booking, a client portal, or handles more than just cleaning? Jobber.

Quick Verdict

Use ZenMaid if you run a residential maid service under 15 cleaners and want cleaning-specific features — tip tracking, room-level client notes, visit reminders — at the lowest price in this category. ZenMaid stays significantly cheaper than Jobber through a 15-person team.

Use Jobber if your business is growing, you need online booking through your website, you have property manager clients who need a self-service portal, or you plan to add services beyond maid cleaning. Also use Jobber if ZenMaid's payment processing has caused problems during your trial — Jobber's payment processing is more reliable based on user reviews.

How Each Tool Works on a Real Maid Service Job

ZenMaid

Regular client profile stores: entry code, preferred cleaning products, room-specific notes ("skip the home office on Tuesday visits"), known preferences (folded toilet paper, beds made a specific way), and tip history. Assigned cleaner opens the app and sees all of this before they arrive. After the cleaning, the cleaner logs the tip received. The client gets an automatic reminder 24 hours before the next visit. No admin involvement needed between visits.

Jobber

Client books through your website booking widget. Recurring job is set up with all standard notes. Client gets a confirmation and can manage their account through the self-service portal — view upcoming visits, request changes, pay invoices online. Property managers use the portal to oversee multiple properties. After each job, the cleaner marks done from the app, the invoice generates automatically, and a payment reminder goes out if needed. No room-level cleaning notes — client notes are at the job level only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZenMaid or Jobber better for a small maid service?

ZenMaid for a pure residential maid service under 15 cleaners where price and cleaning-specific features matter most. The per-seat pricing stays dramatically cheaper than Jobber through a team of 15, and the tip tracking and room-level client notes are native. Switch to Jobber when you need online booking from your website, a client portal, or start adding services beyond maid cleaning.

At what team size does it make sense to switch from ZenMaid to Jobber?

Price isn't the trigger — ZenMaid at $79/mo for 15 cleaners is still cheaper than Jobber's Grow plan at $199/mo. The switch makes sense when features become the gap: when you need online booking (not just a form), when you have commercial clients or property managers who expect a self-service portal, or when you're adding services that ZenMaid's cleaning-only focus doesn't handle. Most cleaning businesses switch around 10–15 cleaners when those feature needs emerge.

Does ZenMaid work in Canada?

Yes — ZenMaid is available to US and Canadian maid service businesses. Pricing is in USD. The tool works identically regardless of location. Jobber is headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta, and has particularly strong Canadian market presence if that's a factor in your decision.

What's the real difference between ZenMaid and Jobber for scheduling?

Both handle recurring job scheduling, automatic reminders, and cleaner assignments. The meaningful difference: ZenMaid stores room-level client preferences that follow every visit automatically — a cleaning-native feature that Jobber doesn't match. Jobber's scheduling is more flexible and connects to online booking and a client portal where clients can manage their own appointments. For a straightforward residential maid service, ZenMaid's scheduling is everything you need. For a business where clients want to self-manage or where bookings come through your website, Jobber's scheduling infrastructure is more capable.
